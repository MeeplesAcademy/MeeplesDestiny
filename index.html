Personal Challenge

To complete all 11 dungeons in Destiny 2 — Solo Flawless — on all 3 characters.

How It Started

My daughter introduced me to Destiny, then Destiny 2. I joined D2 right before Forsaken dropped and I loved the Shattered Throne dungeon. Back then it was very hard for me. Then the second dungeon, Pit of Heresy, came out with Shadowkeep, and I loved spending time in there.

My daughter wanted the Harbinger title, which required a solo run of Pit. Once she accomplished that, I was inspired to try it. Once I got that done, I decided to try for Solo Flawless. But that was back when you could dupe the orb in the Chamber of Suffering (oh it was!). Later, after Bungie patched the orb-duping in Pit, I thought, "I should go back in and try to solo flawless that legit without needing that dupe." So I did (after some deaths, and figuring out Trinity Ghoul was my best friend in the Chamber of Suffering). Once I had that confidence, I went for Shattered Throne, which was a bit easier by then. And once I had Throne done, I thought, "I want to accomplish this on all 3 characters." So within just over a week, I had that achievement and it felt great.

Now, I had to try Pit on Warlock and Hunter, too. And once that was done, I knew it was now my personal challenge to "Conquer" all the dungeons on all 3 characters.
